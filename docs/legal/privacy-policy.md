This Privacy Policy explains how **[Your Legal Entity / "SwipeHire"]** ("we,"
"us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SwipeHire mobile
application and related services (the "Service").

## 1. Information we collect

**You provide:**

- **Account information** — your email address and a password (stored only as a salted hash; we never store your plaintext password).
- **Profile information** (optional) — name, headline, bio, location, and links (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio) you choose to add.
- **Reviews you write** — ratings and text about companies.
- **Outreach messages** — the subject and body of messages you send to recruiters through the Service, with your email set as the reply-to address.

**Generated through your use:**

- **Activity** — which jobs you swipe on (left/right), saved jobs, and applications you initiate.
- **Technical data** — basic device and log information needed to operate and secure the Service.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 2. How we use information

- To create and operate your account and authenticate you.
- To build your job feed and remember your swipes and saved jobs.
- To display reviews and let you submit your own.
- To send recruiter-outreach emails you choose to send.
- To secure the Service, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Third parties and subprocessors

We share data with service providers strictly to operate the Service:

- **Hosting & database** — our API and database providers (e.g., Render and Neon) store and process your account and activity data.
- **Email delivery** — when you send recruiter outreach, the message and your reply-to email are transmitted through our email provider (e.g., Resend) and delivered to the recipient inbox you target.
- **Job listings** — job postings are sourced from public employer job boards (e.g., Greenhouse).
- **Company reviews data** — employer review content may be retrieved from third-party data providers (e.g., the Real-Time Glassdoor Data API). This content originates from those platforms and their users, not from us.
- **Recruiter contacts** (if enabled) — company contact lookups may use a third-party enrichment provider (e.g., Hunter.io).
- **App platform** — the app is built and distributed using Expo and the Apple App Store / Google Play.

When you send an outreach message, you are disclosing your message and reply-to
email address to the recipient. We are not responsible for how recipients use it.

## 4. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where GDPR applies, we process data to perform our contract with you (operating
your account), based on your consent (optional profile data and outreach you
send), and for our legitimate interests (security and improving the Service).

## 5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete
your account, we delete your account, profile, swipes, saved jobs, applications,
and outreach records. Reviews you have written may be retained in de-identified
form (detached from your account) so they remain useful to others, unless you
request their removal.

## 6. Your rights and choices

- **Delete your account** at any time in the app (Account → Delete account), which permanently removes your data as described above.
- **Update your email or password** in the app (Account).
- Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or restrict processing of your data. To exercise these, contact us at **[privacy@yourdomain.com]**.

## 7. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), and
access tokens are stored in the device's secure storage. No system is perfectly
secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

## 8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16 (or the minimum age required in
your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## 9. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the
United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such
transfers.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated"
date and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Questions or requests: **[privacy@yourdomain.com]** · **[Your Legal Entity, mailing address]**.
